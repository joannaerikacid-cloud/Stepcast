Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Stepcast ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Stepcast, based in Newbury, United Kingdom. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Stepcast is the data controller for the personal information we collect. We provide self-guided audio walking tours accessible via our website at stepcasttours.com.

Contact us: stepcasttours@gmail.com

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Purchase data — when you buy a tour through Lemon Squeezy, they collect your email address and payment information on our behalf. We receive a record of your purchase but do not store your card details.
  • License key usage — when you unlock a tour using a license key, we verify it against Lemon Squeezy's API. We do not store your key permanently on our servers.
  • Device data — our tours use your browser's local storage to remember that you have unlocked a tour on your device. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Location data — if you use the GPS feature, your location is used only on your device to show your position on the map. We do not collect, store or transmit your location to our servers.
  • Contact data — if you email us, we will store your email address and the content of your message to respond to your enquiry.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To process your purchase and deliver your unlock code
  • To verify license keys and unlock tour content
  • To respond to customer service enquiries
  • To improve our tours and service

We do not use your data for marketing without your explicit consent. We do not sell your data to any third party.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the following legal bases under UK GDPR:

  • Contract — processing necessary to fulfil your purchase and deliver the service you have paid for
  • Legitimate interests — responding to enquiries and improving our service
  • Legal obligation — where we are required to retain records for tax or legal compliance

5. Third Parties

We use the following third-party services:

  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing and license key management. They act as our Merchant of Record and handle payment data under their own privacy policy.
  • Vercel — website hosting. Our site is served via Vercel's infrastructure.
  • Wikipedia API — we use Wikipedia's public API to display photographs of locations. No personal data is shared with Wikipedia.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain purchase records for 7 years as required by UK tax law. Emails are retained for as long as necessary to resolve your enquiry, and deleted thereafter. Device-local data (your unlock status) is stored in your browser until you clear your browser data.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, email us at stepcasttours@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Our website uses browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to remember your tour unlock status on your device. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for stepcasttours.com.

9. Complaints

If you are unhappy with how we handle your personal data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at stepcasttours.com/privacy.html. We will notify customers of significant changes by email where we hold your email address.
